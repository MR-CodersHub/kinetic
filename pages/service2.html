// Replacement Inquiries

Q// What is Safe Drive-Away Time (SDAT)?

Safe Drive-Away Time is the minimum time a vehicle must remain parked after windshield installation to allow the polyurethane adhesive to fully cure and bond. Our adhesive requires a strict 45-minute cure time.

Q// Do you use OEM or aftermarket windshield glass?

We use OEM-quality glass that meets or exceeds all federal vehicle safety standards. This ensures proper light transmission, thickness, and curvature matching your vehicle's original glass specifications.

// SERVICE TIMELINE

Our Windshield Replacement Process

01 // EXTRACTION

Remove Old Glass

Carefully cutting the old urethane seal and extracting the cracked windshield without scraping paint.

02 // PREPARATION

Frame Cleaning & Prime

Trimming old urethane bead, cleaning dust/moisture, and applying protective black primer to prevent rust.

03 // ADHESIVE

Polyurethane Application

Applying a uniform bead of high-grade quick-curing polyurethane adhesive to ensure a leak-proof seal.

04 // INSTALLATION

Set & Lock Glass

Setting the new glass in place, securing the side mouldings, and performing initial safety camera scans.
