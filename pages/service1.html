// Repair Inquiries

Q// Can any size windshield crack be repaired?

Generally, chips smaller than a coin and cracks shorter than 6 inches can be safely repaired. Larger damage typically requires a full replacement.

Q// Will the chip completely disappear after repair?

The repair will significantly improve the visibility of the chip (by 80% to 90%) and restore the structural strength of the glass, though a tiny spot may remain visible.

// SERVICE TIMELINE

Our Chip Repair Process

01 // PREPARATION

Clean & Prep

Removing loose glass fragments and moisture from the chip center using specialized probes.

02 // VACUUM

Air Extraction

Applying a vacuum tool to draw all trapped air out of the fractured glass layers.

03 // INJECTION

Resin Infusion

Injecting our high-viscosity liquid polymer resin into the crack under pressurized conditions.

04 // CURING

UV Solidification

Applying a UV curing lamp to harden the resin, followed by scraping and polishing the surface smooth.
