Q// How long does a mobile windshield replacement take?

The physical glass replacement takes roughly 45 to 60 minutes. However, your vehicle must remain parked for an additional 45 minutes to allow the quick-cure polyurethane adhesive to bond securely. This ensures your vehicle meets safe drive-away standards.

Q// What areas do your mobile service vans travel to?

Our certified technicians operate fully equipped mobile vans across Chennai and nearby suburbs, dispatching directly to service hubs in Avadi, Sholinganallur, Ambattur, and T. Nagar.

Q// Will my vehicle's safety features (ADAS) still function properly?

Yes. We perform a mandatory ADAS safety camera calibration immediately following a windshield replacement. This ensures your lane keep assist, emergency braking, and other automated safety features are aligned to OEM factory specifications.

Q// How much does windshield repair or replacement cost?

Windshield repair starts at ₹1,500. Replacements vary depending on your vehicle's year, make, model, and whether your glass has acoustic, solar, or head-up display properties. We work directly with insurance providers to cover costs with low or zero out-of-pocket expenses.
