Why Safe Drive-Away Time (SDAT) Matters for Your Windshield

Published: February 3, 2026 // By Kinetic Certified Technicians

When replacing a windshield, the quality and application of the adhesive are critical. The windshield is a key structural component of your vehicle, contributing up to 60% of the cabin roof strength in rollover accidents and keeping the passenger airbag properly deployed. Tech-advanced polyurethane adhesives require a strict Safe Drive-Away Time (SDAT) to cure to a safe density level.

"Driving a vehicle before the windshield adhesive has fully cured can cause the glass to shift, resulting in air/water leaks, or worse, detachment in a collision."

Our mobile technicians use premium, quick-cure polyurethane adhesives that guarantee a safe drive-away time of exactly 45 minutes. This means you can get back on the road quickly, with full confidence that your windshield is structurally sound and certified safe by AGSC guidelines.
