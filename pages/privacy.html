// 1.0 Information We Collect

When you schedule an auto glass service or request a quote, we collect your name, email address, phone number, physical address/service location, vehicle details (year, make, model), and insurance policy details.

// 2.0 Location Data & GPS Tracking

For mobile service operations, our dispatch system processes your address to assign our technicians. Real-time GPS tracking coordinates for service vans are used solely to calculate arrival times (ETAs) and are stored securely for service validation and safety monitoring.

// 3.0 Insurance Claims Coordination

If you choose to file an insurance claim, your policy information, vehicle details, and damage photos are securely shared with your insurance provider or claims administrator (such as Safelite Solutions, GEICO, or Progressive) to coordinate direct billing.

// 4.0 Cookies & Preferences

We use browser cookies to remember your display preferences (such as light vs. dark mode theme settings). We do not sell your personal information or track your browsing activity across other websites.
