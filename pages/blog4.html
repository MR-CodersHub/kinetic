Troubleshooting Common Safety Camera Issues After Glass Changes

Published: May 22, 2026 // By Kinetic Certified Technicians

After replacing your windshield, you might occasionally notice safety warning lights on your dashboard, or find that your vehicle's lane departure warning is unresponsive. In almost every case, these issues stem from a misaligned ADAS camera. If the camera was not calibrated, or if the calibration was interrupted, the vehicle's computer will lock out safety functions to prevent erratic behavior.

"Dashboard alerts or lane assist failures after glass replacement are direct indicators of an alignment offset that needs immediate professional calibration."

To resolve these issues, our technicians hook up specialized diagnostic tools directly to your vehicle's OBD-II port. We scan for diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs), clear calibration faults, and perform a precise recalibration using dynamic in-motion road processes or static physical target boards. This guarantees all safety systems are fully operational.
